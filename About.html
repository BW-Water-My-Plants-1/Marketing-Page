About Us!


Be foliage savvy by taking care of all of your plants
with a single app! We are passionate about keeping
your plants happy and healthy! We help set daily
reminders with alarms on all of your mobile devices
so that you can go about your day with no worries
that your indoor and outdoor plants will get the water
they need so they can grow and succeed!
